About Us

We are an independent sourcing team based in China, dedicated to supporting overseas buyers in industrial procurement.
We work as your long-term sourcing partner and on-the-ground team—helping you identify reliable suppliers, manage procurement execution, and maintain control over quality, cost, and delivery. Rather than acting as a trading company, we represent your interests throughout the sourcing process, providing transparent, practical support for direct factory sourcing in China.

Why we exist

Today’s China supply chain offers massive opportunities—but also overwhelming complexity.
With thousands of factories and intermediaries, it is difficult for overseas buyers to identify reliable suppliers, verify capabilities, and access truly competitive resources.
Many buyers also lack the ability to stay in China long-term to manage sourcing, quality, and delivery.

How we support you

We act as your long-term sourcing support team in China, helping you navigate supplier selection, verification, and execution.
From identifying the right manufacturers to managing production and delivery, we provide continuous, on-the-ground support—so you can maintain control without being physically present.

Our team

We are a lean team composed of local Chinese sourcing professionals and internationally experienced partners.
This allows us to combine deep supply chain knowledge with clear cross-border communication, ensuring efficient execution and alignment with global business practices.

Our goal

Our goal is simple: to help you source from China with full control—achieving more reliable suppliers, better pricing, lower risks, and long-term supply stability.

What problem we solve

Many international buyers rely on trading companies when sourcing from China. While this provides access to suppliers, it often leads to limited transparency, unclear factory background, and weak control over pricing, quality, and delivery.
At the same time, most buyers cannot travel to China frequently to verify suppliers, inspect production, or handle on-site coordination. This creates a gap between decision-making and actual execution.

How we fill this gap

We act as your on-the-ground sourcing team in China, bridging the gap between remote decision-making and local execution.
Instead of trading products, we work alongside you throughout the sourcing process—helping you access real manufacturers, verify capabilities, and maintain control over production and delivery.

What we actually do

Our work is execution-focused and covers key stages of sourcing:
• Supplier identification based on product specifications and target pricing
• Factory verification, including business registration, production capability, and past export records
• Sample evaluation and technical requirement alignment
• Price comparison across multiple suppliers to avoid overpricing
• Production follow-up to ensure timelines and specifications are met
• Quality inspection coordination before shipment
• Delivery and logistics coordination with clear communication

How we are different

We do not act as a trading company.
We do not take commissions from suppliers.
We are not tied to any factory.
Our role is to represent the buyer’s interest at every stage of the sourcing process.

Who we work with

We typically work with:
• Importers and distributors
• Equipment and parts buyers
• Cross-border e-commerce sellers
• Small and mid-sized procurement teams without a China presence

What You Gain

• Direct access to real manufacturers
• Better pricing through comparison
• Reduced sourcing risks
• Full visibility without being in China

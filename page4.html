Independent China-Based Sourcing Team

We are a lean, independent sourcing team based in China, combining local industrial knowledge with international communication experience.Independent. Transparent. Not tied to any factory. Our goal is to help international buyers build safe and efficient long-term supplier relationships in China.

Team Identity & Service Position

Are you a trading middleman who earns profits from product price differences?

No. We are a buyer-exclusive international procurement execution team, representing only the buyer's interests throughout the process. We do not earn product price differences, accept factory rebates, or have any hidden fees. We only charge clearly displayed service fees, and all factory quotations are fully transparent and verifiable.

Are you a company or an individual? Is a small team reliable?

We are a small international operational team based in Wuhan, equipped with professional sourcing, risk control, and on-site execution personnel, proficient in English and Russian bilingual communication. Our service processes are standardized, all materials are traceable, and consultations are responded to within 24 hours. We are more flexible and cost-effective than traditional traders, and more secure than individual services.

What categories of procurement services are you good at?

We focus on four core industrial tracks, with years of experience and a pre-screened database of compliant factories: auto & auto parts, agricultural machinery, machine tools and generator sets. We do not undertake unrelated categories to ensure service professionalism.

What is the size of your team? Can you undertake the needs of multiple customers at the same time?

We are a streamlined and efficient international team with less than 5 core members, with clear division of labor (2 sourcing, 2 on-site execution, 1 risk control). We can undertake the needs of 10-15 customers at the same time, ensuring that each customer's service is not perfunctory and the time limit is not delayed.

What are your advantages compared with individual procurement agents?

Compared with individual agents, our advantages are more prominent:
① Team collaboration avoids service interruption caused by limited personal energy;
② Pre-screened factory database for higher efficiency;
③ Improved risk control system to better avoid procurement risks;
④ More professional bilingual services for smoother connection.

Do you only serve overseas buyers? Can domestic buyers cooperate?

We mainly serve overseas buyers, solving procurement pain points for four major categories in China. Domestic buyers with sourcing and audit needs for the four categories are also welcome with the same standards and pricing.

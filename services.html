Procurement Advisory | Standardized Q&A Framework

This section answers frequently asked questions about cross-border industrial procurement, with customized one-on-one consultation available. Adhering to buyer-only service principles and local operational capabilities, we deliver transparent, independent, margin-free solutions. Focused on supply chain risk control and efficiency improvement, we fully safeguard compliance and procurement interests for global buyers.

Team Identity & Service Position

Are you a trading middleman who earns profits from product price differences?

No. We are a buyer-exclusive international procurement execution team, representing only the buyer's interests throughout the process. We do not earn product price differences, accept factory rebates, or have any hidden fees. We only charge clearly displayed service fees, and all factory quotations are fully transparent and verifiable.

Are you a company or an individual? Is a small team reliable?

We are a small international operational team based in Wuhan, equipped with professional sourcing, risk control, and on-site execution personnel, proficient in English and Russian bilingual communication. Our service processes are standardized, all materials are traceable, and consultations are responded to within 24 hours. We are more flexible and cost-effective than traditional traders, and more secure than individual services.

What categories of procurement services are you good at?

We focus on four core industrial tracks, with years of experience and a pre-screened database of compliant factories: auto & auto parts, agricultural machinery, machine tools and generator sets. We do not undertake unrelated categories to ensure service professionalism.

What is the size of your team? Can you undertake the needs of multiple customers at the same time?

We are a streamlined and efficient international team with less than 5 core members, with clear division of labor (2 sourcing, 2 on-site execution, 1 risk control). We can undertake the needs of 10-15 customers at the same time, ensuring that each customer's service is not perfunctory and the time limit is not delayed.

What are your advantages compared with individual procurement agents?

Compared with individual agents, our advantages are more prominent:
① Team collaboration avoids service interruption caused by limited personal energy;
② Pre-screened factory database for higher efficiency;
③ Improved risk control system to better avoid procurement risks;
④ More professional bilingual services for smoother connection.

Do you only serve overseas buyers? Can domestic buyers cooperate?

We mainly serve overseas buyers, solving procurement pain points for four major categories in China. Domestic buyers with sourcing and audit needs for the four categories are also welcome with the same standards and pricing.

Three Service Models 

What does the free consultation include? Are there any hidden fees?

The free consultation has no hidden fees or mandatory purchase requirements.

It typically includes an initial discussion of your requirements, basic verification of one potential factory, preliminary sourcing guidance, indicative cost estimation, and key risk insights — helping you clarify your procurement direction before making any formal decision. 

Will factory contact details be provided?

Whether factory contact details are provided depends on the service model you choose.

For independent sourcing support, we provide screened supplier contact details, quotations, and relevant documents, allowing you to communicate and negotiate directly.

For full-process managed sourcing, we typically handle supplier communication on your behalf to ensure efficiency, pricing stability, and smooth execution.

Can I request direct contact with suppliers during the project?

Yes — this can be arranged based on the project stage and actual needs.

In full-process sourcing, centralized communication helps reduce misunderstandings, improve efficiency, and ensure consistent quality control.

If direct communication is necessary, we can assist in arranging introductions or coordinated discussions at an appropriate stage. 

How is the service fee determined?

The service fee is flexibly determined based on the specific project, mainly considering:

Order volume
Product type and complexity
Customization requirements
Supply chain difficulty
Level of service required

In general:

Standard, high-volume orders → lower service fees
Smaller or more complex projects → higher service involvement and cost

All pricing will be clearly discussed and confirmed before cooperation begins.

Can I choose only part of the service?

Yes. You can select individual services such as supplier verification, factory audits, or sourcing support based on your actual needs.

We will evaluate each request individually and match the appropriate service scope accordingly. 

How are previous services handled if I continue cooperation?

If you choose to continue cooperation after an initial service, we will structure the overall arrangement in a practical and fair way.

Previous work and fees may be reasonably considered within the overall project scope, depending on timing, workload, and level of involvement.

All details will be clearly communicated before proceeding further.
